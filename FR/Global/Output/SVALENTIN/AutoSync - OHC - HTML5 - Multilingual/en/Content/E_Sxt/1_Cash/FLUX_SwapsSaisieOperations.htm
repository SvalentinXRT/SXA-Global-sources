Transaction Input

You can enter your swaps directly from MLT Financing/Investing Transactions.

E.g.:

A financial manager enters a borrowing at fixed-rate of 4.000000%.

As they anticipate a rate decrease, they set up a swap through which they receive the fixed rate (4.000000%) and pay the floating rate (2.067000%).

On the swap’s first day, the profit would correspond to 4.000000% - 2.067000% i.e. 1.933000%.

In the transaction edition window, click IRS (Interest Rate Swap), to access the swap creation.

After saving both the underlying and the rate swap, click Schedule to display the three amortization tables.

Our financial manager:

– pays the fixed rate (4,000000) for their financing

– pays the fixed rate (4,000000), swap’s interests asset.

– pays the floating rate (2,067000), swap’s interests liability.

Your environment now includes a new column showing whether swaps have been set up for your transactions.

In our example:

The value for the floating rate goes from 2.067000% to 5.000000%.

Our manager reckons that this trend is going to last and that the swap hence costs more than the original 4.000000%.

They decide to set up a new swap through which they receive the floating rate and pay the fixed rate.

To set up a new swap, you must proceed with the following:

1. Back-office Validation

2. Selection of Change Participants, in Actions.

For the new version of the financing, just like for the previous one, click IRS to enter another swap.

After saving both the underlying and the rate swap, click Schedule to display the three amortization tables.

Our financial manager:

– pays the fixed rate (4,000000) for their financing

– pays the fixed rate (5,000000), swap’s interests asset.

– pays the floating rate, (3,000000), swap’s interests liability.

On swap setup dates, the net position is:

Nature On Swap1 date On Swap2 date
Financing - 4.000000 - 4.000000
Swap1 asset 4.000000 4.000000
Swap1 liability - 2.06700 - 5.000000
Swap2 asset   5.000000
Swap2 liability   - 3.000000
Net Position - 2.06700 - 3.000000
Profit/Loss 1.933000 1.000000
