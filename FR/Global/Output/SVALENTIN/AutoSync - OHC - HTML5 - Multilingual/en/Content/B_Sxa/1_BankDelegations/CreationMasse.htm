Creating Delegations by Batch

Templates smoothen the creation and monitoring processes for delegations; their creations are therefore recommended.

Delegation Templates consist of all the elements mentioned in the introduction except for Delegatees.

Watch video 

 Full Screen

Delegation Templates are created and saved for their future exploitation during batch creation of delegations.

If one or more delegatees are assigned to a template, then one unit delegation is generated per delegatee, transaction type and bank account.

So, if a template is associated with two transaction types, two bank accounts and two delegatees, then eight unit delegations are generated, i.e.:

2 transaction types * 2 bank accounts * 2 delegatees = 8 unit delegations

Once the delegation has been created, it is completely independent of the template. So modifications to the template have no impact on the delegations that were created from it.

Every created delegation has its own life cycle and can be individually modified.
1-List Signature Templates
  1. In the Bank Delegations menu, select Delegations.

The Create contextual menu is displayed on the right.

  1. Click Create by Template and the template name from the selection dialog box.

The Delegations - Create by Template screen appears.

  1. Complete the sections and select the delegatees.

  1. Click Save in the contextual menu on the right to save this new delegation.
2-List Signature Templates

Delegations with 2-Delegatee Lists may be created by batch through a new template.

  1. In the Bank Delegations menu, select Delegations.

The Create contextual menu is displayed on the right.

  1. Click Create with 2-Signature Template and the template name from the selection dialog box.

The Delegations - Create with 2-Signature Template screen appears.

  1. Complete the sections and select the First Signature Delegatees and Second Signature Delegatees.

  1. Click Save in the contextual menu on the right to save this new delegation.
