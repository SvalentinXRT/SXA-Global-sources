Setup

In , the flow codes dedicated to swaps must be created in Transactions.

  1. Click the Create button to open the creation dialog box.
  2. Enter the Receipts/Expenses flow codes you want to associate with rate swap transactions, then enter the corresponding rate swap asset/liability transactions.

  1. Click the Create button to open another creation dialog box.
  2. Enter the transactions for the setup of buy/sell swaps with which the asset/liability transactions are associated.
