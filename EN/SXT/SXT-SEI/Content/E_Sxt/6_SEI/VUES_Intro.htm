Views

Views are based on existing processes. All the fields are made available to the view, categorized as dimensions, measures or descriptions.

The view organizer consists of three main sections:

  • Groups: contains the drill-down structure of the view and the visual order of the fields
  • Pivots: creates a series of column values based on the selected fields placed under this section
  • Columns: contains the list of fields displayed in the view, not grouped.
