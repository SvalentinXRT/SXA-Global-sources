Command Center

The Command Center pane allows you to:

  • Open all available views (worksheets, gauges, maps, charts and dashboards)
  • Select and execute view-related operations
  • Organize processes and views in different folders

Double-click on a view from the Command Center to display the corresponding data in the Navigation area and in the selected layout (Worksheet, Chart, Gauge, Map or Dashboard).

The content of the Command Center depends on the profile user. It only shows the processes and views that a user is authorized to access.
