SXT

is a management module for interco. debts from range, designed for multinational companies which want total control over their subsidiaries' transactions and the reduction of these transaction number and costs.

This solution is totally independent of the banks, which ensures maximum flexibility to Financial Directions.

Using amounts to pay and to collect to calculate net positions for the various entities enables multinational companies to minimize the number of transactions to only one (payment or disbursement) by participant.

Multinational companies have many production and sales divisions, they therefore generate many inter-company invoices. enables a substantial reduction in payment numbers through the invoices centralization, and the subsequent calculation of the net result among participants.
