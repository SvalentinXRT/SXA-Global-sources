Modifying Invoices Cycles

This action enables you to modify cycles assigned to internal and external invoices by batch-processing.

The filter enables you to select the invoices to modify.

After this selection, a list is displayed from which you can assign the invoice to another cycle with the Modify Cycle function.

For each invoice modification, these two elements are checked:

  • Status
  • Netting Cycle

If the invoice does not have the Notified or Refused statuses, or if the cycle originally assigned and the modified one are the same, the modification is rejected.
