Managing Remittances

When you need to view, modify, delete or generate remittances afterwards, you must do it through the Remittance menu.

The table below shows the possible actions depending on the remittance.

Status Possibilities
Constituted Remittance (preparing Transfers) Generate the remittance
Delete orders
Delete the remittance
Generated Remittances (formatted, ready to be sent) View the remittance
Archive the remittance

In addition, you can always edit the remittance slip for the bank and the confirmation letter for the beneficiary, whatever the remittance status.

The Select ‏Remittances page opens up when you click the button enabling you to choose between, for example, the constituted and generated remittances. The All Remittances Display (constituted, generated, archived) is selected by default.

When you validate the criteria, the <Transfer Type> Remittances page opens up.

Modifying/Viewing Remittances

After selecting the remittance, click to open the <Transfer Type> Remittances page.

All the useful elements appear in two group boxes:

  • The Remittance group box recaps the applicant name, the branch name, the remittance status (generated or constituted) and specifies whether the bank gives the details of each transfer by beneficiary on the account statement (global debit) or not.
  • The Orders group box displays all the orders of the remittance. From this list, you can only view the information on the screen.
 Generating Constituted Remittances

When remittances were not processed directly from the Process by batch option of the Validation menu, they can be processed later on.

  1. Select a remittance from the list (Domestic, International, Treasury Remittances, etc.) displayed after selecting one of the options of the Remittances menu.
  1. Click in the toolbar.
  2. Select Generate from the dropdown list.
