Exceptional Procedures for Transfer Modification/Deletion

During the processing of your orders, errors may occur on validated transfers, in spite of all the control and validation steps.

However, we know that a transfer linked to a remittance or that is To Validate cannot be deleted. But you can modify or delete this transfer by changing its status back to To Validate, through a specific procedure.

Before deleting the unique reference of a transfer, you need to "unbind" the remittance to which the transfer is linked. For more information on the deletion of constituted remittances, refer to the Deleting Remittances topic.
Modifying/Deleting Validated Transfers
  1. Memorize the number of the transfer to modify.
  2. From the pane, go to Common Elements.
  3. In the Common Administration menu, select the Payment & Receipt section.
  4. Click Exceptional Procedure in the Linked Tasks menu.

  1. Complete the Number field, then click OK.

  1. Click OK again in the dialog box to go back to the homepage of Common Elements.
When returning to the module, you can see that the transfer affected by the exceptional procedure is now in the list of Transfers To Validate. So you can still modify it before validation.
