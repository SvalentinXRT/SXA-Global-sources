Permanent Features

, like any other modules of the range, has the same standard approach and process. The intuitive interface and functions facilitate their usage.

For creation, modification or deletion, here is the chronology of the actions to take:

  • selection of the general command granting access to the type of elements (for example: Transactions, then Treasury Transfers...),
  • definition of the potential selection criteria to filter the relevant elements (for example: the international treasury transfers as opposed to the treasury transfers),
  • selection of the element(s) to process,
  • selection of the command launching the process (for example: International Transfers - Create),
  • entry modification or viewing of the information,
  • validation.

In addition, every time you create a transaction, you can re-use information previously entered - if you saved them as a template - or save the elements that you have just entered: they can be used as a template later on.
