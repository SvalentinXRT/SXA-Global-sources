Dealing Ticket

This document recaps all the charcateristics of the payment, as they appear in the dialog box where details are entered and viewed.

So, if you edit the dealing ticket from the payment list, it only includes the minimum of details, whether the payment is complete or not.

On the contrary, if you edit the dealing ticket from the Transfers list, it includes all the fields. Depending on the order status, these fields are completed or blank.

Whatever the access to payments, the Ticket command in the contextual menu generates the document in the Reporting module.
