Setting up

Once you set up the basic entities in , you can go on with the specific setup in . You can do it from the menu.

This function enables you, among other things, to set up your preferences for the Treasury Transfers management.

Here are the different steps for the setup of :

  • The setup of Profiles, requiring the identification of the formats and protocols used by your payment branches.
  • The creation of Constitution Rules, so that the batch constitution of transfers to remit complies with certain rules.
  • The setup of Client Identifiers, provided during the remittance to the banking institution.
  • The setup of the Economic Types, which are required for the validation of the transfer order, when the applicant of the beneficiary is a non-resident.
To view the same module names as those specified in this documentation, select the option under the accordions, on the lower and left part of the screen. If you do not select , the module names may be different from the documentation.
