Transfers

Once you set up all the elements required for the full operation of the module and that you integrated potential payment request files, you can get to the core by starting processing Payment Orders.

Processing gathers the following actions:

  • entering payment requests,
  • completing payment requests with the information required for them to become Treasuy Transfers or selecting Treasury Transfers if they have been already created,
  • monitoring the life cycles of Transfers through their status changes (To Modify, To Authorize, To Validate, Prepared...),
  • validating transactions back-office,
  • constituting remittances,
  • sending remittances,
  • editing linked documents (dealing tickets, confirmation letters, remittance slips).

The Cash Ledger offers a quick view of cash flows generated in the module.

This sectiontopic presents the essential information required for the Transfer Management, from the payment request creation to their remittance, including all the required intermediary actions.

The back-office processing and the corresponding administrative steps are also explained in this section.
