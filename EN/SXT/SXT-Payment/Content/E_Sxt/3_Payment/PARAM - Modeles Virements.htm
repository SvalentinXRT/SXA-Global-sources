Transfer Templates

Templates can be compared to the templates of Word letters. If you regularly issue transfers for a same provider, you can create a transfer template. These templates are organized by transfer type on the Payment Elements page.

Setting up Transfer Templates
  1. To access this function, in the pane, select the Payment Elements option in the folder of the module.

The setup page for Receipts Elements is displayed. Click the relevant template link, in the Templates section.

The list of created templates is displayed.

  1. Select your template and click the View button to display its contents.
  2. Click OK to go back to the page of the template list.

You can also delete templates via the list, modify them, print them and export them to Excel.

Templates are created by entering transfers. You need to save this transfer as a template by clicking the corresponding button.
