Reports

The module provides you with two types of reports.

  • The Budget Entries Ledger report enables you to generate a budget entries ledger.
  • The Budget Sheet report enables you to generate a file presenting in a sheet:
  • A version of the Treasury Budget (initial version, revised version or last saved version depending on your selection)
  • The flows saved in
  • The discrepancy between these flows and the Treasury Budget
  • The Compare exercises report enables the comparison between Budget Exercises: between various exercise versions, entity groups, or years, along with the display of amounts and discrepancies.

You can also generate a consolidated view of Treasury Budgets at company group level, by using the consolidated display.

Report filters enable you to set up selection criteria for transactions, as well as the report layout.

You can save the reports in different formats, print and send them.
