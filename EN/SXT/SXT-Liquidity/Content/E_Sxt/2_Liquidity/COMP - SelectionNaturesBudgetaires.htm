Selecting Budget Codes

You can modify the selection of budget codes to be included into the exercises comparison in two ways.

  • You select another budget sheet designer in the corresponding filter.

Refer to the process described below for more information.

  • You modify the structure associated with the selected designer when adding budget codes.

For more information on the setup of budget sheet structures, see the Budget Structures topic.

  1. In the management page for Exercises Comparison, click the Change button in the Filters toolbar.
    The setup page for the selected filter is displayed.

  1. In the Designer area, click the button to display the list of the set up designers, then, in the contextual window, double-click a line to select the corresponding designer.

The codes and descriptions of the selected designer are displayed in the area.
