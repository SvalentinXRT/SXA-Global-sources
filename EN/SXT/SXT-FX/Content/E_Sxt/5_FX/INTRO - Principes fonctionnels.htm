Functional Principles

is designed to enable the follow-up of the exchange position using the functions described below.

Setup

The setup functions enable you to adapt the operational mode of the module to the specificities of the exchange risk management in your company.

 Commercial Forecasts

Commercial Forecasts which are completely integrated into your software, take into account the forecasted exchange rates and market exchange rates. You can then assess the relevance of your foreign exchange hedge.

 FX Transactions

FX Transactions are entered with all the information required for a secure and comprehensive administrative processing.

 Administrative Management

The back-office validation, the setup of counterparties and bank instructions are associated with your data to ensure an accurate administrative management. The locking of the back-office modification function reinforces the security of this management by preventing any transaction modification that would not be part of the strictly defined authorization process.

 Notification Management

The notification management includes dealing tickets, confirmation letters and transfer orders and can be completely automated to improve significantly the security of your administrative management.

 Analysis

With its positions, reports and ledgers, simplifies the detailed analysis of your exchange risk and currency transactions.

 Data Import and Export

This function enables you to import the commercial transactions created within another application, and to export FX transactions into other applications, which enhances your productivity.

 Integration within application suite

uses the same flow basis and the same account balances than the module. Its data are completely consistent with the Cash data.
