Currency Borrowings and Loans

The Currency Borrowings and Loans are managed through .

You can create, update, validate back-office, carry forward and delete currency borrowing and loan transactions.

For more information on these functions, see the module documentation.

As for , it enables you to manage the exchange risks linked to these transactions.

  • Loan and Borrowing Transactions are integrated into the FX Position Worksheet, along with FX and Commercial Transactions. See the FX Position Worksheet topic for more information.
  • The Currency Loans/Borrowings Ledger enables you to view all the recorded transactions. See the Currency Borrowings/Loans Ledger topic for more information.
