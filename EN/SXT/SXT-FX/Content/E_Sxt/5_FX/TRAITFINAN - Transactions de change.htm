FX Transactions

offers a complete transactional management of the various hedging instruments, supplied according to your needs. You can:

  • enter the Spot and Forward Exchange Transactions, the Spot/Forward and Forward/Forward Swap Transactions,
  • settle, exercize and carry forward the Forward Contracts,
  • link the Forward Exchange Transactions to Commercial Forecasts,
  • validating transactions back-office,
  • edit the linked documents (dealing tickets, confirmation letter, payment order).

The Cash Ledger offers a quick view of cash flows generated in the module.

This topic presents the essential of the information required for the FX transactions management, from the transactions creation to the actual status, including all the required intermediary actions.

The back-office processing and the corresponding administrative steps are also explained in this section.
