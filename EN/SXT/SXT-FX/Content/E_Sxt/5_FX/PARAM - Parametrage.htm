Module

The elements that need to be set up in , are listed below. For more information on the setup process, see the module documentation.

Market
  • Banks (used for bank account setup)
  • Country (used for bank account setup)
  • Currencies
  • Calendars: referenced in the currencies setup, this is the broker calendar used for the calculation of the various transaction dates depending on the worked days for the currency quotation.
 Entities and Bank Accounts
  • Companies
  • Counterparties: banks or companies, bank groups or company groups with which a transaction can be created
  • Additional Counterparties: banks and companies with which relationships are occasional
  • Third Parties (if needed): third organizations used for commercial forecasts
  • Bank Accounts: bank details of the companies used during transactions settlement
  • Counterparty Accounts: bank details of the counterparties and other counterparties used during the transaction settlement
 Transactions and Flows
  • Transaction Types: coding of transaction types and association with the different types of cash flows
  • Spot Exchange Transactions: spot buy, spot sell
  • Forward Exchange Transactions: forward buy, forward sell
  • Swap Transactions: forward buy of spot swap, forward sell of spot swap, buy of forward/forward swap, sell of forward/forward swap
  • Borrowings and Loans: currency borrowing, currency loan
  • Commercial Transactions: receipt forecasts, disbursement forecasts
  • Budget Codes (if needed): natures of the forecast flows used for Commercial Forecasts
 Grouping Numbers
  • Link Numbers: references enabling the grouping of transactions for common analysis and processing
  • File Numbers (if needed)
  • Portfolio Numbers (if needed)
