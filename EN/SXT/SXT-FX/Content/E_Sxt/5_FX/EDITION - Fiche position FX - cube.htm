Cube Configuration

You can set up the information to be displayed in the cube.

  • Categories

These are the categories of amount to display: commercial transactions, financial transactions, hedging, etc.

  • Facts

These are the values to display for each category of amount: consolidation amount, amount, exchange rate.

  • Dimensions

These are grouping criteria for the amounts.

Amounts can be grouped by:

  • company, currency, period and category if the detailed view for the amounts was set up,
  • company, currency, period and net hedging if the global view for the amounts was set up,

The time interval for the period can be defined in the FX Position Worksheet. It can be a day, a week, a decade, a fortnight, a month or a quarter.

You can also cancel all these filtering settings.

Select the amount categories to be displayed
  1. In the FX Position Worksheet, click the header of the Category column, on the top left of the table.

For more information on how to access the Position Worksheet, see the FX Position Worksheet topic.

The Category contextual menu appears.

  1. Enable the options corresponding to the amount categories that you want to display and disable the ones that you do not need.
  2. Click the button to validate your selection.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed according to your settings.

 Selecting Facts
  1. In the FX Position Worksheet, click the Facts button in the OLAP toolbar.

For more information on how to access the Position Worksheet, see the FX Position Worksheet topic.

The Facts contextual menu appears.

  1. Enable the options of the facts to display and disable the others.

When clicking a line, you can view its information in the lower part of the window, called Fact Properties.

  1. To modify the fact description, click the Value field on the Snapshot line of the Fact Properties. Enter a description in this field and press the Enter key.
  2. Click the button to save your configuration, then to validate it.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed according to your settings.

 Selecting Dimensions
  1. In the FX Position Worksheet, click the Dimensions button in the OLAP toolbar.

For more information on how to access the Position Worksheet, see the FX Position Worksheet topic.

The Dimensions contextual menu appears.

  1. Enable the options of the dimensions to display and disable the others.
  2. Click the button to validate your selection.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed according to your settings.

 Organizing Grouping Criteria for Amounts

In the Position Worksheet, you can choose four grouping criteria for amounts, you can apply them to the lines or to the columns and you can choose the grouping order: companies, currencies, periods, amount categories.

The following snapshot shows a configuration example.

In the example above, here are the grouping criteria for the facts (columns):

  • the currency is the first grouping criterion,
  • the period is the second grouping criterion.

These criteria are displayed on one line at the top of the table, from left to right following the grouping order and in the column on the left of the table, from top to bottom following the grouping order.

The line under the toolbars displays the disabled grouping criteria. In this example, we are talking about the company.

To enable or disable these criteria, click the criterion to select and drag and drop it to the first line or the first column of the table.

When you enable a criterion, make sure you organized the criteria in the relevant grouping order.

During drag-and-drop, you can see a green cursor which indicates where the criterion will be located when dropping.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed as soon as you modify the grouping criteria setup.

 Filtering Values of Grouping Criteria

You can filter the values of the grouping criteria for the amounts.

  1. Double-click the criterion to filter on the line displaying the grouping criteria at the top of the table.

A contextual menu appears.

  1. Disable the options of the lines that you want to exclude from your selection.
  2. Click the button to validate your selection.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed according to your settings.

 Cancelling Filtering

You can cancel all the filtering settings on the dimensions, facts and categories.

In the Position Worksheet, click the Cancel Filter button in the actions toolbar.

The Position Worksheet is refreshed according to your settings.
