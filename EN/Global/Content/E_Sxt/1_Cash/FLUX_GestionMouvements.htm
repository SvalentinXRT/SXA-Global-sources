Transaction Management

First of all, the accounts balances must be initialized in before any forecast input in .

Creating forecasts enables you to anticipate balances, and therefore to make the appropriate decisions.

Entries are considered as Forecasts until the bank register them and they appear on Bank Statements. There are two types of forecasts:

  • Original Forecasts: their amount or date is uncertain, for instance a forecast of a transfer expected from a client.
  • Confirmed Forecasts: these data are reliable, they will be shortly, actual for instance, cheque remittance or issued transfer.

You can supply with forecasts from a number of sources:

  • By import from G/L
  • By direct input
  • By entry into the Ledger
  • By entry into the Cash Position Worksheet
