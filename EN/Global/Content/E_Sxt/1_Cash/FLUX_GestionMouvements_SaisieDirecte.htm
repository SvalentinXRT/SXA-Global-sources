Direct Entry of Forecasts

Entries are considered as Forecasts until they appear on Bank Statements. Forecast status is Original Forecast when its amount or date is uncertain.

With the bank entries transmission through , the module enables the reconciliation between the entries and the forecasts you entered. During this reconciliation, the forecast status turns to Actual.

Batch-input of forecasts is ensured through the automatic integration of data from the accounting system. But you can also enter the data manually in . This kind of input enables the integration of the receipts and expenses forecasts. Forecasts are either original or confirmed forecasts of commercial flows.

The financial flows, Financings and Investings, are to be entered from the Financial Transactions dashboard.

If you own FX module, entering currency buys and sells in it directly generates the corresponding flows in .
