Netting Balance

This report enables you to check the smooth operation of the netting process.

The net positions of each participant are displayed for a cycle and a simulation.

This report is only available for internal invoices in the netting currency.

You may also display the used rates and generate two types of netting balance reports. They depend on the rate used:

  • Average Rate
  • Sell/Buy (Ask/Bid) Rates

When the Average Rate is used, the total of the invoices to pay equals the total of the invoices to collect. Reversely, when Sell/Buy (Ask/Bid) Rates are used, there is a discrepancy between both these totals. During the netting process, the invoices to pay for a participant are calculated according to the buy rate, whereas the invoices to collect are calculated according to the sell rate, which explains the difference.
