Selecting Remittances

The Remittance Selection page is automatically displayed when you click the button after selecting one of the links of the Remittance menu.

This function is used:

  • If a batch of orders should not be sent right after being constituted. (since if it had to, you would use the Process by Batch link from the Validation menu). Thus, constitution and generation are sequenced one after the other.
  • If a batch should be deleted in order to modify its transactions.

None of these fields is mandatory. If you do not want to filter your remittances, just click OK without completing the tabs.
Completing Remittances Selection Page
  1. Specify the institution designated to pay for the transactions in the Issuing Branch field.
  1. Complete the Applicant field to display the transactions incurred by a given company.
  1. In the Status group box, select the Constituted option to display the remittances to generate, the Generated option to display the remittances to be transmitted to the bank, or the Archived option to display the remittances that have been generated and transmitted to the bank.

In the Filter By group box, you can intervene on two levels: the remittance date and the currency.

  1. Enable the Remittance Date option if you want the selection to include the remittances performed within a specific date range. You now have two options to complete this date range in the From and To fields:
  • You can either enter them directly.

In this case, enter the values without paying attention to ' / ' or to the thousands for the year.

  • Or you can select them from the calendar.

Click the arrow button in the first area, and select your date, it then appears in the area. If the transaction date's year and month are not the current year and month (default display), use the left or right arrows to change the year or the month.

  1. Select Currency to activate the button and select a currency in order to filter the remittances according to it.
