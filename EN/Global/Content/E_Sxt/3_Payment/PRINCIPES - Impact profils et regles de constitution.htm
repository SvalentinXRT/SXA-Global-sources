Profiles and Constitution Rules

Constitution Rules refine Profiles. They are not priority elements. To constitute Remittances, the module first creates batches according to formats, and potentially refines these batches according to the constitution rules corresponding to the banks' commercial agreements, if they comply with the formats. In other words, Constitution Rules are linked to banks' specificities, whereas Profiles are the first basic rules.
