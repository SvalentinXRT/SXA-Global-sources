Transfers Follow-up

To be remitted to the bank, a transfer order must be at least To Validate. But to obtain this status, it needs to be fully completed.

For the integration of payment requests originating from your G/L, after defining their transaction type, you must complete them in the module.

So, you can complete them from the function dedicated to the corresponding type, i.e. Domestic Transfers, International Transfers, Treasury Transfers, etc., and the status of these transfers consequently changes from To Complete to To Validate.
