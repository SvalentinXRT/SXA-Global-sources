Selecting Transactions

Every action focused on transaction management, Payment Requests or Transfers, must go through the Selection page.

This page is displayed when you click the following links in the Validation menu:

  • Validate by Batch
  • Authorize by Batch
  • Process by Batch

As well as those links in the Transactions menu:

  • Change by Batch
  • Prepare Funds Transfers

This is an extremely valuable tool when dealing with large volumes of transactions, as it facilitates access to the required information. It enables you to enter filtering criteria to display customized lists of transactions. From this list, you can then proceed to modify, delete, validate, create and remit transactions.

The Selection page consists in two tabs that refine your request:

  • The General tab that enables the selection of basic criteria. These criteria are built around the first information level related to a payment request or order.
  • The Advanced tab that goes further by specifying the progression status of the orders.
None of these fields is mandatory. If you do not want to filter your transactions, just click OK without completing the tabs.
Completing Selection Page
  1. In the General tab, select a payor for the Payor field by clicking , if you want to view the transactions assigned to a specific applicant.

  1. Select a beneficiary if necessary for the Payee field by clicking . When the Payor field has not been completed, the selection includes the transactions issued for the same Beneficiary, whatever their applicants.
  2. Complete the Currency field with the internal code for the currency in which your transactions are expressed.
  3. Complete the Transaction field if you want only one transaction type.
  4. Enable the Transaction Date option if you want the selection to include transactions performed within a specific date range. You now have two options to complete this date range in the From and To fields:
  • You can either enter them directly.

In this case, enter the values without paying attention to ' / ' or to the thousands for the year.

  • Or you can select them from the calendar.

Click the arrow button in the first area, and select your date, it then appears in the area. If the transaction date's year and month are not the current year and month (default display), use the left or right arrows to change the year or the month.

  1. To filter transactions according to an amount range, enter the minimum and maximum values in the From and To fields respectively.
None of these fields is mandatory. If you do not want to filter your transactions, just click OK without completing the tabs.
  1. Click the Advanced tab to refine the selection by:
  • Transaction Number: enter the digits only.
  • Reference
  • Status: the types of statuses enabled by default depend on the link selected to access the Selection page. For example, for the Process by batch link, the default statuses are: To Validate, Validated and Authorized.
  • Source: the Local option selects the transactions that were entered, the Imported option selects the transactions imported from the G/L.
  • Transaction Type
  1. Click OK to validate your request.

The list of the transactions that meet your selection criteria, is displayed.

You can still go back to the criteria using the button of the toolbar.
