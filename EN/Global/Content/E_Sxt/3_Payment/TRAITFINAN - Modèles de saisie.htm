Data Entry Templates

Data Entry Templates are used to save pieces of information entered while creating transactions. Once you saved these information elements as a template, you simply need to select it for another creation. Most of the fields are then automatically completed.

The only fields left to be defined are Reference, Amount (including the currency) and Transaction Date. When using a template, you have to complete these fields and check for potential errors to correct.

Five template categories are available:

  • Payment Request Templates,
  • International Transfer Templates,
  • Commercial Transfer Templates,
  • Domestic Transfer Templates,
  • Treasury Transfer Templates,
  • International Treasury Transfer Templates.

Using templates is not mandatory but it simplifies and speeds the entry.
