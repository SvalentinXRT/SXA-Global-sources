Managing Payments

This topic deals with the management functions for the exchange risk, apart from the setup, the reports, the data import and export: i.e. the creation and process of Payment Requests and FX Transactions.

Here is the order of the payment process:

  1. You create a payment request.
  2. Then, you enter the information required for this payment to become a transfer.
  3. The transfer is validated.
  4. Eventually, this transfer is included within a batch, before being remitted to the bank.
