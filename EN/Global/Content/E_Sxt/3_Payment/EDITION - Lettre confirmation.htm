Confirmation Letter

This document is a mail to inform the beneficiary on the imminent processing of the transaction by the bank.

Confirmation Letters are completely adaptable to your working environment. They specify whether the applicant is also the payor, whether there is an intermediary bank between the benficiary's bank and the issuer's bank, and so on.

The Confirmation Letter option is available from the transfers (if they are Prepared or Remitted) and the remittances. This document is also available when you generate remittances.

It contains the following information, among other things:

  • the date of the letter,
  • the execution date for the order,
  • the transfer amount,
  • the name of the bank for the applicant,
  • the account ID on which the money is taken,
  • the potential instructions of the payor for its bank.
If you launch this document from the remittances, you get as many letters as orders in the remittance.
