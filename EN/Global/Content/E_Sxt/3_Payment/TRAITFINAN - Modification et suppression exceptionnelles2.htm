Deleting Remittances

During the processing of your remittances, errors may occur on constituted remittances, in spite of all the control and validation steps.

The application enables you to delete remittances with the Constituted status, through a specific procedure.

Deleting Constituted Remittances
  1. Memorize the number of the remittance to delete.
  2. From the pane, go to Common Elements.
  3. In the Common Administration menu, select the Payment & Receipt section.
  4. Click Exceptional Procedure in the Linked Tasks menu.

  1. In the Transaction field, select the Remittance option button.
  2. Complete the Number field, then click OK.

  1. Click OK again in the dialog box to go back to the homepage of Common Elements.
When returning to the module, you can see that the remittance affected by the exceptional procedure has been removed from the Remittances list. Thus, you can create another Remittance with the relevant orders (modified if needed) to remit to the bank. For more information on exceptional transfer modification, see the Exceptional Transfer Modification/Deletion topic.

Once your remittance is Generated or Archived, you can still delete it.

Deleting Generated/Archived Remittances

For these statuses, deletion consists in two steps.

First:

  1. Memorize the number of the remittance to delete.
  2. From the pane, go to Common Elements.
  3. In the Common Administration menu, select the Payment & Receipt section.
  4. Click Exceptional Procedure in the Linked Tasks menu.

  1. In the Transaction field, select the Remittance option button.
  1. Complete the Number field, then click OK.

  1. Click OK again in the dialog box to go back to the homepage of Common Elements.
When going back to the module, you can see that your remittance appears in the list of Constituted Remittances.

You can now delete it, which is the second step.

  1. Select the remittance whose status has just been changed to Constituted, in the remittance menu of the corresponding transfer type.
  1. Click in the toolbar.
  2. Click Yes in the dialog box to confirm the deletion.

  1. Click OK to validate the action and go back to the remittance list. You can see that the remittance has disappeared from the list.

On top of the remittance deletion for which only the constituted remittance disappears (and not the transfers that formed it), you can purge the payment transactions.

Purging Payment Transactions
  1. From the pane, go to Common Elements.
  2. In the Common Administration menu, select the Payment & Receipt section.
  3. Click Purge Payment and Receipts Transactions in the Linked Tasks menu.

  1. Define the various display criteria.
  2. In the Transaction tab, you can select the types of the transactions that you want to delete.
  3. Click OK.

  1. Select the transfers to purge, then click Purge.

  1. Click Yes to validate your action.
