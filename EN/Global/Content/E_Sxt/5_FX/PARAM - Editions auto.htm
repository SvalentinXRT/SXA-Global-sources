Automatic Reports

This setup enables the automation of reports and documents when FX Transactions and Commercial Forecasts are entered.

  1. To access this function, in the pane, select the Automatic Reports option in the folder of the module.

The setup page for the automatic reports is displayed.

  1. Enable the Dealing Ticket option, if you want to print the dealing ticket right after the transaction creation.
  2. Enable the Confirmation Letter option, if you want to print the confirmation letter right after the transaction's back-office validation.
  3. Enable the Payment Order option, if you want to print the order payment right after:
  • the settlement (including the automatic settlement following the carry forward or the early exercise) of a forward transaction or a swap forward leg,
  • the back-office validation of a spot transaction or a swap spot leg.
The manual reports remain available, even though the report automatic management is selected.

To modify the document templates, see the Modifying Document Templates topic.
