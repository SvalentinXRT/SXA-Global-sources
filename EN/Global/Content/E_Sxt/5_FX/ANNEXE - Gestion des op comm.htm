Management Modes for Commercial Transactions

Three management modes for Commercial Transactions are available:

  • Detailed, Reconciliation Mode,
  • Global, Budget Decrementation Mode,
  • Global, Budget Decrementation Mode.

For more information on the configuration for these three modes, see the FX Configuration topic.

In Detailed, Reconciliation Mode, the corresponding flows are posted in the Treasury with the "Confirmed Forecast" status, as soon as a commercial forecast is created or modified.
