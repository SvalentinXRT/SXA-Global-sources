Detailed Reconciliation

In Detailed, Reconciliation Mode, the applied principle is a reconciliation of the treasury with the forecasts. The payments made in the module decrement the forecasts saved in , amount by amount and forecast by forecast (a strict correspondence is established).

Receipt forecast for month M: 100

Consisting of 3 forecasts:

  • 30, the 12th of month M
  • 10, the 17th of month M
  • 60, the 25th of month M

Received payments:

  • 30, the 12th of month M
  • 10, the 17th of month M

New position of forecasts: 60

The first two forecasts are reconciled, the third one remains at 60 at the 25th of month M. After the reconciliation, the Cash Position Worksheet is consequently updated.
