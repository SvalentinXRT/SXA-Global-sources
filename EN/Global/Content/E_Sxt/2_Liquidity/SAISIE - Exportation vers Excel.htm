Exporting to Microsoft Excel

You can export your Operational Budget Sheet and your Treasury Budget Sheet to Microsoft® Excel®.

Only the total amounts in the cells are exported. The distribution of amounts in transactions and the calculation formulas are not exported.

To export the budget amounts to Excel:

  • click the Export to Excel button in the actions bar,
    - or -
  • right-click an editable cell of the budget sheet, then, in the contextual menu, select the Export to Excel option.

Microsoft Excel application is started and displays a document with two tabs: one presents the Operational Budget amounts, the other presents the Treasury Budget amounts.
