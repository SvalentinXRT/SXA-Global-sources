Worksheet

Worksheets are users' favorite views from .

The Command Center enables you to view a Worksheet. When you select a worksheet, it is then displayed in the main window.

The various groups are displayed at the top of the screen, the active current group is highlighted in green.

You can modify the order of the groups by dragging and dropping them wherever you want.

To focus on a level of detail, select the line and use the Drill down function.

The corresponding data are displayed.
