Filters and Display Modes

enables you to separate the selection of data to be displayed (filter) from the way data are displayed (view mode).

Filtering Data

Filters enable the selection of fields and their data to be displayed. Example: Companies, Banks, etc.

Here are the available operators to create Filters: Equals, Not equal, Contains, Like, In, Is Null et Is Not Null.

Example: Company Equals Sage France and Bank Equals BNPP.

Only one filter can be defined on screen.
Display Modes

The display mode enables the definition of columns, sorting, grouping, details, aggregates, grouping aggregates and graphs.

  • Columns
    • Show/hide columns with + and -.
    • Define the display by moving columns with your cursor.
  • Sorting
    • By column
    • By sorting order
  • Grouping
    • By line with + and -.
    • By grouping order by moving columns with your cursor
Only one display mode can be defined on screen.
Quick Filter

The main fields for standard filters are also available for the Quick Filter.

The Quick Filter may be saved as a standard filter.
