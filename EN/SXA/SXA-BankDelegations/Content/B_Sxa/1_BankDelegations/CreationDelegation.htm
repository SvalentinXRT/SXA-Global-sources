Creating Delegations

Bank Delegations consist of the following elements:

  • Delegator(s)
  • Delegatee(s)
  • Bank Account(s)
  • Transaction Type(s)
  • Signature Type(s)
  • Limit
  • Date

provides you with two creation methods for Bank Delegations: Batch Creation (by template) and Unit Creation (one delegation per delegatee).
