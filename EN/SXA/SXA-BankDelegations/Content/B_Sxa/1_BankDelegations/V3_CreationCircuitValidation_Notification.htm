Notification

This sending function notifies by emails the users involved in validation requests.

You can activate notifications when creating Validation Rules:

  1. In the Validation Workflow section, click the Add button under Validation Levels.
  2. Once you defined the Level and the type of Validation Rule, click Select to select the users.
  3. Validate and save your validation rule.
Do not forget to complete the E-mail field in the Contact tab of the User record.
