Letter Templates

Letter Templates offer total control over the contents of your letters. After adding contents to the templates, you obtain a final letter, ready to be sent to banks.

Each part of letter templates can be created and viewed in the Designer.

You can modify the whole layout of Delegation Letters, as well as organize their info.

You can use the variables from the Insert HTML dropdown list to insert the corresponding elements (company names, account numbers, etc.) from the database into the relevant parts of your letters.

With the Group by action, in Delegation Data section, data may be grouped according to various criteria: Limit, Currency, Account, Signatures, Signature Groups, Operation Type, Delegatees and Employee Group.

Several levels of grouping may be associated. So you can group data by Account and by Delegatee.
