Generation Rules

Generation Rules enable you to prepare the content of the letters to be generated.

When you use letter templates, the letters contents appear on screen as they do on the final document.

Generation Rules may be set up by Company, Branch, Account, Operation Type and Employee Group.

Example of Generation Rule:

Creating Generation Rules

  • Description: Generation Rule for ACCOUNT A
  • Company: COMPANY 1
  • Accounts: ACCOUNT A
  • Bank Branch: BRANCH 1
  • Transaction: Transfer Orders

When using the rule, you can retrieve all the delegations corresponding to COMPANY 1 + BRANCH 1 + ACCOUNT A + Transfer Orders and group them into a same letter.

Result: Letter Content

  1. DELEGATEE 1, ACCOUNT A, Transfers Orders, Single Signature, 1,000,000.00 Euro
  2. DELEGATEE 2, ACCOUNT A, Transfers Orders, Single Signature, 1,000,000.00 Euro
  3. DELEGATEE 3, ACCOUNT A, Transfers Orders, Single Signature, 1,000,000.00 Euro

 

From the Letter Generation Rules window, you can select one or more generation rules, and you can launch the Generate letter by rule action.

The letter content is then automatically generated.

Letter contents may also be generated from the Delegations window through the selection of the relevant delegations and the launch of the Generate Letters action.
